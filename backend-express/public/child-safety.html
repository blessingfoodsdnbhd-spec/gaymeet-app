Child Safety Standards

Meyou (com.meetupnearby.app) — our standards against child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

Developer: Jacky Teh · Last updated: 2026

1. Adults only (18+)

Meyou is a dating and social application intended exclusively for adults aged 18 and over. Accounts are restricted to users 18+. We do not knowingly allow minors to create accounts or use the service. Any account found to belong to a minor is removed.

2. Zero tolerance for child sexual abuse material (CSAM)

We have a strict zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), including child sexual abuse material (CSAM), grooming, and any sexualization of minors. Such content and conduct are strictly prohibited and result in immediate account termination.

3. Prevention and detection

  • Age restriction (18+) enforced at sign-up and in store targeting.
  • In-app reporting: users can report any user or content directly within the app.
  • Blocking: users can block any other user at any time.
  • Content moderation: reported users and content are reviewed and removed when in violation.
  • Account termination for any confirmed CSAE violation.

4. Reporting child safety concerns

Users can report child safety concerns in-app through the report function on any profile, chat, or piece of content. Reports are reviewed promptly.

You may also contact us directly at xiuyi8008@icloud.com.

5. Compliance and reporting to authorities

Meyou complies with all applicable child safety laws. We cooperate with and report apparent child sexual abuse material to the relevant regional and national authorities, including the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) where applicable.
