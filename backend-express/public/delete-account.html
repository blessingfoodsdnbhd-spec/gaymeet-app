Delete Your Meyou Account

Meyou (developer: Jacky Teh) — how to permanently delete your account and data.

Delete in the app (recommended)

  1. Open the Meyou app and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings → Account.
  3. Tap Delete Account and confirm.
  4. Your account and associated data are permanently deleted immediately.

Delete by email

If you can't access the app, email support@meyou.app from your registered address with the subject "Delete my account". We will process the request within 30 days.

What is deleted

When you delete your account, the following are permanently removed:

  • Your profile (name, email, age, bio, interests, preferences)
  • All photos (profile, private, topic personas, moments, chat images)
  • Your matches, conversations, and messages
  • Your likes/swipes, follows, topic personas and unlock grants
  • Photo-access requests and your login sessions
  • Gift and payment records associated with your account

What is kept

We do not retain personal data after deletion, except where required by law (for example, transaction/tax records may be retained by our payment processors — Google Play / Apple — for the period mandated by applicable financial regulations). Such records are held by those processors, not by Meyou, and are not used to identify you within the app.
